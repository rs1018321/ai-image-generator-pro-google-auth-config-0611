## 1. Information We Collect

| Data Type | Details | Purpose |
| Account Data | Email, password hash | Account registration & login |
| Payment Data | Billing name, last 4 card digits (Creem) | Payment & tax processing |
| Usage Data | Prompts, generated images, IP, device | Service delivery & moderation |
| Cookies | Session ID, locale | Maintain login & language prefs |

## 2. How We Use Data
Operate the Service; prevent fraud and abuse; comply with law; send product updates you opt-in to.

## 3. Content Moderation
Prompts and images are automatically screened by OpenAI Moderation and other classifiers. Suspicious content is logged for up to 90 days and may be reviewed by staff.

## 4. Data Sharing
We share data only with:
- (a) Creem for payment processing
- (b) cloud vendors under GDPR-compliant contracts
- (c) authorities when legally required

We never sell personal data.

## 5. International Transfers
Your data may be processed on servers outside your country; we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

## 6. Your Rights
Access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and withdrawal of consent. Email support@coloring-pages.app to exercise rights; we respond within 30 days.

## 7. Security
TLS in transit, encryption at rest, least-privilege access, and annual penetration tests.

## 8. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn of such data, we delete it within 72 hours.
